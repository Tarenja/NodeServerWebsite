Frostbolt develops high-quality console, PC, and online role-playing games focused on rich stories, unforgettable characters, and vast worlds to discover. Since 2017, Frostbolt has created some of the world's most critically acclaimed titles, including Man VS Universe™, Wild Wasteland Wanderer™, and Magic Vikings™. Frostbolt operates in Toronto (Ontario, Canada), Montreal (Quebec, Canada), and Austin (Texas, United States).

Frostbolt’s vision is to create the most amazing game worlds ever to grace screens.
